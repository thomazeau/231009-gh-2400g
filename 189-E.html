How can you walk with God (8 min.)

3 ways

1 - Walking in the truth

3 John 3 calls this “Walking in the truth”  = a way of life for us = listening to his voice and letting God direct our steps
true story
Raudel Rodriguez - Cuba :: w2012 1/1 "The Bible changes lives"

Young man raised in a poor neighbourhood of Havana, wanted to rebel against the political system in his land
One day with a classmate, they attacked a police officer to steal his gun. The policeman was badly injured in the head.
They were put in prison and condemned to death by firing squad. Only 20 years old.

Raudel states: " In the loneliness of my prison cell, I wondered: ‘Why is there so much injustice in the world? Is this life all there is?’
It was at this point that I met some of Jehovah’s Witnesses, who were in prison for their religious beliefs. I was impressed by the Witnesses’ courageous yet peaceable behaviour. Though wrongly imprisoned, they were not angry or bitter. I decided to read the Bible on my own. When I finished, I realized that Jehovah’s Witnesses are the only people who behave like the early Christians."

Raudel began studying the Bible, but realized that he needed to make some drastic changes in his life. He cleaned up his speech, quit smoking and stopped taking sides on political issues.
"One of the hardest changes was learning to control my temper. I still pray for self-control in this regard. I have been greatly helped by such Bible verses as Proverbs 16:32, which says: “The one slow to anger is better than a mighty man, And the one controlling his temper than one conquering a city."
Listening to that one verse changed his life. Raudel was baptized as one of Jehovah’s Witnesses in a barrel of water in the prison.

He says: "I am a happy man, serving God along with my wife and our daughters. Sometimes I think back to that young man who was about to die, and I appreciate how much I have gained since then.
Raudel chose to walk in the truth, and is a happy man because of that.
